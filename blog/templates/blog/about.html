About Me

I'm an undergraduate student at Northeastern University (and previously from George Mason University) studying computer science and english. I'm currently working with the MIT Media Lab for the summer on Fluid Interfaces. I have also interned with Zulip and Valti.

See my resume for my full list of experiences and accomplishments.

My main hobbies include programming, hacking, writing, reading, and staying up far too late researching whatever's interesting. You'll probably get a better sense of me and my endevours by reading my blog.

I also enjoy to teach the things I love to learn. I have been an undergraduate teacher assistant for the Computer Science department at GMU and a training and certification instructor for TOPS at GMU. And, I've volunteered at various workshops with The MIT Educational Studies Program, the Boston Python User Group, and OpenHatch.
